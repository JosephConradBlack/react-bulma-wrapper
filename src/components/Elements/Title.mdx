# Title

There are two types of headings:
* Title
* Subtitle

<Playground>
    <Title>Title</Title>
    <Subtitle>Subtitle</Subtitle>
</Playground>

## Typography Helpers

Titles support the full suite of [Typography Helpers](/bulma/typography).

The size property is particularly of use:

<Playground>
    <Title size={1}>Title</Title>
    <Title size={2}>Title</Title>
    <Title size={3}>Title</Title>
    <Title size={4}>Title</Title>
    <Title size={5}>Title</Title>
    <Title size={6}>Title</Title>
    <Title size={7}>Title</Title>
    <Subtitle size={1}>Subtitle</Subtitle>
    <Subtitle size={2}>Subtitle</Subtitle>
    <Subtitle size={3}>Subtitle</Subtitle>
    <Subtitle size={4}>Subtitle</Subtitle>
    <Subtitle size={5}>Subtitle</Subtitle>
    <Subtitle size={6}>Subtitle</Subtitle>
    <Subtitle size={7}>Subtitle</Subtitle>
</Playground>
